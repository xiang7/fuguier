The Personas




Qingxia Lin, Traveler

Qingxia loves traveling and she is always looking forward experiencing new culture, food and everything. Recently she is planning going to San Francisco. Instead of doing research online and following handbooks as always, this time she wants someone local to show her the best way to explore this place. She is hoping that her trip could be unique and special.



Daniel Tucker, Local

Born and raised in the Bay Area, Daniel loves and knows this place more than anybody else. Whenever he has friends visiting, he can always find out great ways to show them around the area. In his spare time, he loves surfing, exploring nice restaurants, discovering the unique sides of the city and so on. He is willing to share his experience and time, and making friends with people across the world.
